About Tyana

Affordable glow, graceful details, and jewellery made to be worn often.

Our Point of View

Tyana Jewels is built for women who want that polished final touch without overthinking the price or the styling. The brand blends modern silhouettes with a soft luxe mood that feels wearable, giftable, and social-ready.

What We Prioritize

Anti-tarnish materials, lightweight comfort, and pieces that move easily from lectures to office looks to dinner plans. The aim is simple: grace in the design, glow in the finish, and confidence in the wear.
